Souvlaki Greek Recipe

Description

Souvlaki is a popular Greek dish made with marinated grilled meat, typically served on skewers. It is often accompanied by pita bread, tzatziki sauce, and a variety of vegetables.

Souvlaki is a delicious and flavorful dish that is enjoyed by many people around the world.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts or pork tenderloin, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon black pepper
  • 8 wooden skewers, soaked in water for 30 minutes

Steps

  1. In a bowl, combine olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, dried oregano, dried thyme, dried rosemary, salt, and black pepper. Mix well to make the marinade.

  2. Add the chicken or pork cubes to the marinade and toss to coat. Cover the bowl and refrigerate for at least 1 hour, or overnight for best results.

  3. Preheat grill to medium-high heat.

  4. Thread the marinated meat onto the soaked wooden skewers.

  5. Grill the skewers for about 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, until the meat is cooked through and slightly charred.

  6. Remove the skewers from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes.

  7. Serve the souvlaki with pita bread, tzatziki sauce, and your choice of vegetables.
